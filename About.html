Introducing Bartex IT Consulting

Lester Bartek Kuzmicz IT Consulting is a premier provider of professional IT consulting services with over 20 years of industry experience. Led by Lester Bartek Kuzmicz, a seasoned Systems Engineer, our team brings a wealth of expertise in managing and implementing enterprise-level computer systems on the Wintel platform.

At Lester Bartek Kuzmicz IT Consulting, we specialize in the administration and implementation of network domain infrastructure on MS Server 2000-2016, as well as the deployment of Windows servers and workstations. Our proficiency in scripting languages such as PowerShell, Shell batch, C#, Python, and VB enables us to deliver efficient automation, seamless migrations, and effective monitoring solutions.

Throughout Lester's extensive career, he has held diverse technical roles, showcasing his versatility and expertise. As a Systems Administrator, he has played a significant role in administering network infrastructure, implementing cutting-edge technologies such as Microsoft System Center Operations Manager 2012 and SCCM 2012. As a Systems Engineer, he has spearheaded the management of Windows Active Directory environments and successfully executed complex migration projects. Lester has also shared his knowledge and expertise as an Instructor, delivering training on Active Directory administration and PowerShell scripting.

Lester Bartek Kuzmicz IT Consulting has a proven track record of successfully delivering software solutions to a wide range of organizations, including renowned names such as ExxonMobil, Microsoft Canada Corp., RBC, and TD Bank. With our client-centric approach, we ensure that our customers receive expert guidance and make informed design decisions during implementation.

Lester Bartek Kuzmicz, the driving force behind our consulting services, holds a Master of Philosophy (M.Phil) degree and possesses several professional certifications, including Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE), Microsoft Certified Systems Administrator (MCSA), and CompTIA A+ and Network+. His ability to manage multiple projects concurrently, combined with his problem-solving skills and strong communication abilities, makes him a valuable addition to any team.

At Lester Bartek Kuzmicz IT Consulting, we are dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and industry trends. We are committed to providing top-notch IT consulting services that empower organizations to achieve their business goals.

Contact us today to discuss your IT consulting needs and let Lester Bartek Kuzmicz IT Consulting help drive your business to new heights.
